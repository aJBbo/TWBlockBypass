New Repo Submission

Author: BigBoss

Submit your tweak, app or utility and have it hosted on our repo. After a few days or so, if accepted and approved it will be published and appear in Cydia.

CydiaStore is no longer accepting paid items. If you want to sell your software, you need to include your own payments solution.

We are no longer accepting themes.

See this guide for important advice for new developers and tweak makers.

If you have any questions or problems please write me, repo maintainer: optimo at thebigboss dot org

Rules for submission

1) You must own the project and be the developer and rights-holder of the content submitted.

2) No porn or adult apps. No swagbucks style apps where user makes you money by shopping with your ID.

3) Screenshots for any GUI affected are required. If you don’t provide screenshots, your submission could be delayed.

4) Make sure your app is signed with ldid and properly built. We recommend you build with Theos but you can use whatever you want as long as you test in a jailbroken iOS environment. See this guide for how to build and prepare your software for Cydia distribution.

Note: make sure you get a confirmation that the submission was successful on this page! Approvals may take 24 to 48 hours. You will receive email notification regarding its status.
